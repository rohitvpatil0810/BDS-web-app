KNOWLEDGE INTEGRATION

In this project, we aim to develop an interactive wiki knowledge base which is based on explainable AI. With the support of diverse machine learning technologies, the system could provide users with context-specific knowledge for particular interests and scenarios. By interacting with the AI agents, users can also be involved in the knowledge collection and optimization processes during the usage. Finally, With the collaboration of corresponding users, we produce the customized(context-specific), transferable and computationally executable knowledge from the integrated datasets of the relevant domains.

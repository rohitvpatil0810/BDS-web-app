ADDC

ADDC will be a completely new approach to circuit design as currently, no fully automated approaches exist. Due to the trade-offs between circuit characteristics such as size and speed, its impact can be enormous; new, low-powered versions of circuits can be created, and, because of the automated nature of the system, it has the potential to generate massively complex circuits that even skilled humans would find difficult if not impossible to design.
